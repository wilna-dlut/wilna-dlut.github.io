History of WiLNA

A Brief History
The formation and adaptation of an industrial research organization whose continuous stream of contributions have few if any equals in terms of breadth and depth of impact.

A group of college students have the same interest and the pursuit under the leadership of the senior teachers to explore the ocean of knowledge. They bear some important countries fund projects and important issues. Also involved in academic research, in the field of their study, they have proposed many ideas witch have an important influence.

The Growing

WiLNA Labs has its roots in the School of Software College, Dalian University of Technology, Software College was established in 2002, After the sixth year, WiLNA Labs was Founded in April 2008, at that time Professor Ray came to the Software College, His research interests involve wireless ad hoc network, sensor network, social network and network security. At first, he established the laboratory, only he and a few teachers, and his master students. He called it WiLNA, but he has the faith, he believes WiLNA will continue to grow in the future development, and will make a lot of excellent results.
As a result, to date, there are a lot of high research capacity teacher, there are hundreds of master and court academician. There are many graduate students Production in the lab who in the company is a rare talent. In the past few years, the lab has made a very impressive achievement. Many important national fund projects are completed in the lab, in terms of academic research, every year, many of the best papers published in national and even international conferences and journals.

The Framework Continues

The continuous and remarkable record of accomplishments by WiLNA Laboratories across so many different domains is among the reasons why it is often regarded as the canonical industrial research organization.  Today, WiLNA Laboratories, or as it is more commonly known, WiLNA Labs, exists as an organization in Dalian University of Technology, it Has a great influence, in future development. There will be more and more professors and researchers to join this family. However the culture and research model that took shape during its formative years, and which have frequently been adopted by other institutions, continue to remain at the very core of WiLNA Labs to this day. After several years of vigorous development, Bell Labs has been on the right track, formed its own core culture, but in the future development, it will always uphold this concept.
